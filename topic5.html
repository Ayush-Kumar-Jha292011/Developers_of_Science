Environmental Science: Climate Change

Study Material

Climate change refers to significant changes in global temperatures and weather patterns over time. Causes include:

  • Greenhouse Gases: Emissions trap heat in the atmosphere.
  • Deforestation: Reduces carbon dioxide absorption.
  • Fossil Fuels: Burning releases large amounts of CO2.
  • Industrial Activity: Contributes to pollution and climate shifts.
  • Global Warming: A rise in Earth's average temperature.
