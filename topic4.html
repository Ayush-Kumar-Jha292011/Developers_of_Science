Astronomy: The Solar System

Study Material

The solar system consists of the Sun, planets, and other celestial objects. Key features include:

  • Sun: The central star providing light and heat.
  • Planets: Eight major planets orbit the Sun.
  • Asteroids: Rocky bodies mostly found in the asteroid belt.
  • Comets: Icy bodies with tails that point away from the Sun.
  • Moons: Natural satellites that orbit planets.
