Chemistry: Periodic Table

Study Material

The periodic table organizes elements based on their properties. Key concepts include:

  • Groups: Vertical columns with similar chemical properties.
  • Periods: Horizontal rows indicating energy levels.
  • Metals, Non-metals, and Metalloids: Different classifications based on properties.
